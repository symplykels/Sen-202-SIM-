### Problem Summary
High rates of localized campus and community crime, driven by factors such as cultism, drug abuse, poor environmental lighting, and slow manual processes, present a significant safety challenge. The Security Incident Management (SIM) platform directly addresses these challenges by introducing an accessible, centralized digital reporting channel. This prototype serves to eliminate reporting friction, bridge communication gaps, and provide visual situational awareness through localized threat data mapping.
